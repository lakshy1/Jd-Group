WELCOME TO JD Group

DECADES OF AGRICULTURAL
EXPERIENCE

JD Group has built its legacy over multiple decades as one of the trusted agricultural trading and export companies specializing in quality grains and non-basmati rice. With a proven track record and a deep understanding of farming practices, JD Group stands as a bridge between traditional agricultural excellence and global supply reliability.

The company has strengthened its export footprint consistently — shipping premium rice and agricultural commodities to UAE, Nepal, Ghana, Benin, Nigeria and several other African and Middle-Eastern countries. JD Group maintains long-term buyer trust through quality assurance, systematic logistics, and commitment to global food safety.

With a robust network of growers, logistics professionals and international partners, JD Group continues to expand its role as a reliable supplier to global households and distributors.

OUR PRODUCTS

PREMIUM NON-BASMATI RICE RANGE

Our Non-Basmati rice variants are known for their firm texture, natural aroma, and high nutritional value — ideal for African, Middle-Eastern and Asian cuisines. Sourced directly from trusted farmers and processed with utmost care, our grains ensure purity, hygiene and consistent cooking quality.

IR-64 Parboiled Rice

widely demanded long-grain non-basmati rice known for its good cooking quality, fluffy texture, and economical pricing

Jai Shree Ram Rice

premium-grade non-basmati rice known for its clean grains, reliable quality, and consistent cooking performance

Sona Masuri Rice

lightweight, aromatic, premium-quality non-basmati rice known for its soft texture, low starch content for daily meals

5/25/50/75/100% Broken Rice

Cost-efficient, high-energy value, used in breweries, feed and foods. Depending upon requirement.
